Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

"Personal Recap" is a private, non-commercial application built and used solely by its developer (Janik) to retrieve his own public TikTok profile and video statistics (such as follower count, likes, and views) for personal weekly progress tracking.

Data We Access

Via the TikTok API, this app accesses basic public profile information and video engagement metrics belonging to the developer's own TikTok account.

Data Use

Data is used exclusively to generate a private weekly summary for the developer's own personal records. It is not shared with, sold to, or disclosed to any third party.

Data Storage

Retrieved data may be stored locally in the developer's private notes for personal reference only.
